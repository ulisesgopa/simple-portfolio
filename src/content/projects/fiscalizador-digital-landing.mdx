**Fiscalizador Digital** is a mobile application for security officers to efficiently manage traffic violations, replacing physical ticket books. The app integrates automatic license plate recognition, GPS location, and evidence capture with the device‚Äôs camera.

The site was built to promote this application and explain its features to potential users.

## ‚ú® Solution

### Tech Stack
- **Next.js:** For fast and efficient page rendering.
- **TailwindCSS:** For a clean, responsive design.
- **Mobile-First Design:** Optimized for mobile devices first, with full support for tablets and desktops.

### Key Features
1. **Evidence Capture:** Officers can capture images of the scene and automatically add them to the violation report.
2. **License Plate Recognition (LPR):** Automatic recognition of vehicle license plates to save time and reduce errors.
3. **Data Validation & Notification:** Automatic data collection (date, time, location) and easy notification of offenders via email and SMS.
4. **Violation Management System:** A robust system to manage, process, and notify traffic violations for governments.

### User Flow
1. **Capture Evidence:** Officers use their phone‚Äôs camera to capture images of the violation.
2. **Select Violation Type:** Choose the violation type from a predefined list and access related legal information.
3. **Generate and Notify Violation:** After validating the information, officers can generate the report and send notifications to offenders.
4. **Manage Violations:** The system allows governments to process, manage, and follow up on violations efficiently.
